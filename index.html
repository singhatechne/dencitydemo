Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do implants hurt?
Procedure is under local anesthesia; mild soreness afterwards, manageable with painkillers.
❓ How long does teeth whitening last?
6 months to 2 years depending on diet and hygiene. Touch-ups available.
❓ What's the cost of Invisalign?
Starts from NPR 1,50,000; varies with complexity. Consultation free.
❓ Are braces painful?
Mild discomfort after adjustments, usually subsides in few days.
❓ How often should I visit dentist?
Every 6 months for checkup and scaling.
❓ Can I eat after root canal?
Wait until numbness wears off, soft foods for first 24h.
❓ What is laser gum contouring recovery?
Few days of mild sensitivity; immediate improvement.
❓ Do you accept insurance?
Yes, we work with most local and international insurers.
❓ Emergency appointments?
Same-day slots available for pain / trauma.
❓ Child's first dental visit?
Recommended by age 1 or when first tooth appears.
